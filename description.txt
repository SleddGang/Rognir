Whispers echo of a new treasure that has been unearthed in the depths of the dungeon. They say the crown of a forgotten king that holds great power has been groaning in the darkness, yearning to be returned to it's true resting place.

The Frozen King is a small content mod for pre-hardmode terraria which adds the Nordic King, Rognir. He's a dangerous individual who is fast-paced and hard to maneuver.
His two stages provides a variety of attacks that consists of ice spikes, flying dashes, a floating anchor, and more!

This mod adds a total of 1 Boss, 4 Items, 1 Buff, and a new song.
